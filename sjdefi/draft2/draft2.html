San Juan De Dios Educational Foundation, Inc. (SJDEFI) is a private, non-stock, non-profit institution in Pasay City, Philippines — jointly operating a college and a hospital rooted in the Vincentian tradition of compassion and service since 1578.

Run by the Daughters of Charity and inspired by St. Vincent de Paul and St. Louise de Marillac, SJDEFI trains the mind, heart, and hands of its students — empowering them to become the best version of themselves and serve their communities with love.

Allied Health Programs

  • Bachelor of Science in Nursing
    Make a difference in people’s lives by playing a vital role in patient care, health promotion, and disease prevention. Join the Legacy of the Pioneers. Train in our own base hospital and graduate as a globally competitive nurse with Vincentian values.
    🩺Compassion. Care. Leadership.
  • Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology
    Play a vital role in performing laboratory tests and analyzing results to assist physicians in diagnosing and treating patients.Master the Science of Precision. Gain 60 years of quality education with direct access to our own DOH-Accredited Molecular Diagnostic Center.
    🧪Science that saves lives.
  • Bachelor of Science in Physical Therapy
    Become a key player in helping patients recover from injuries and develop personalized rehabilitation programs to improve mobility and alleviate pain. Benefit from diverse internship affiliations with PCMC, Veterans Memorial, and Delos Santos Medical Center.
    🏃Move. Restore. Rebuild.

College of Business and Management

  • Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management
    Learn the skills to lead and manage businesses in the fast-paced hospitality and tourism industry. Lead with Service. Master the 3Cs of Hospitality (Christ-centered, Competent, and Creative) in our competency-based program.
    🏨Service. Experience. Excellence.
  • Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship
    Launch your own business, manage startups, and innovate to create something new. Build a Business with Purpose. Go beyond theory with hands-on experience in product selling and business implementation.
    🚀Ideas into impact.
  • Bachelor of Science in Office Administration
    Manage the backbone of businesses—administrative tasks, office operations, and organizational leadership. The Future of Work. Master current technology-rich office systems and organizational leadership for modern business success.
    📊Organize. Coordinate. Lead.

ADMISSIONS

Start Your Journey Here

Joining the SJDEFI community is straightforward. Follow these steps and become part of a tradition of excellence that spans over four centuries.

1

Submit Requirements

Prepare and submit all required documents to the Registrar's Office or through our online portal.

2

Take Entrance Examination

Schedule and take the SJDEFI Entrance Examination. Results are released within 3-5 working days.

3

Interview & Assessment

Qualified applicants proceed to a personal interview with the department chairperson or admissions panel.

4

Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete enrollment by paying the initial fees and submitting final documents.

Admission Requirements

  • ✓ Original & Photocopy of Form 138 (Report Card)
  • ✓ Original & Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • ✓ Good Moral Character Certificate
  • ✓ 2x2 & 1x1 ID Photos (White Background)
  • ✓ Medical Certificate from Licensed Physician
  • ✓ Certificate of Completion (SHS / ALS graduates)
  • ✓ Honorable Dismissal (Transferees)
  • ✓ Duly Accomplished Enrollment Form
⚠️ Requirements may vary per program. Please contact the Registrar's Office for program-specific requirements and the latest enrollment schedule.
