Features

Everything You Need to Stay on Track

Simple enough for everyday use, powerful enough to handle your entire household's medication inventory.

📊

Real-Time Inventory Tracking

See exactly how many tablets remain for every medicine. A live progress bar and colour-coded status (Good → Low → Critical → Empty) give you an instant visual health check.

🔔

Low-Stock Refill Alerts

Set a custom refill threshold for each medicine. When stock drops below it, you get a push notification before you run out — not after.

📅

Dose Schedule Management

Assign Morning, Noon, Evening, or Night dose slots — or enter a custom daily count. The app automatically calculates consumption and days remaining.

💰

Cost & Expense Tracking

Log purchase prices and restock costs. See your cost-per-tablet, monthly spend estimate, and total amount spent — all without a separate spreadsheet.

➕

One-Tap Restocking

Bought a new supply? Add tablets instantly from the detail screen. Every restock is saved to a timestamped history so you always have a full purchase record.

📋

Full Transaction Log

Every add, update, restock, and delete is logged with a timestamp. Export the log to share with your doctor or caregiver in one tap.

💾

Backup & Data Export

Your data is automatically backed up locally so it survives app updates. Export your entire medicine list as a JSON file to keep a personal copy.

🔒

Fully Offline & Private

All data stays on your device. No internet connection required, no account to create, and no personal data ever sent to a server.

🔕

Alert Snooze

Already aware of a low-stock situation? Snooze the alert for 24 hours so you're not repeatedly reminded about the same item while you wait for your prescription.
